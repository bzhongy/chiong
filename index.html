The Zero-Day Advantage

Harness the power of accelerated time decay and maximum leverage

Zero-day expiration delivers unmatched capital efficiency

  • Accelerated Time Decay: Options lose value exponentially as expiry approaches, creating both opportunities and risks
  • Maximum Leverage: 0DTE options provide up to 2000x leverage compared to 100x for perpetuals and futures
  • Defined Timeframe: All positions settle at the same time each day, no additional margin requirements
  • No Liquidation Risk: Unlike futures or perpetuals, 0DTE options can never be liquidated until expiry. Price manipulation tactics cannot force you out of your position.
